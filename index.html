Welcome at NCS

Welcome to the Website of the Nigeria Customs Service. We sincerely hope that the global trading community, travellers and other stakeholders will find the information provided through this medium useful and timely.

The FRSC is currently headed by Boboye O Oyeyemi, MFR, mni, whose title of Corps Marshal is the highest rank in the Corps ranking system.

Dynamics of Technology have changed the conduct and scope of international trade. As a critical actor in the unfolding development, Customs can not afford to be indifferent. Information dissemination about Customs Operations is therefore considered very vital to trade facilitation in a world that is becoming increasingly technologically - driven.

Careers at NCS

Nigerian Customs Recruitment is done once every year and 2019 won’t be exempted. When the form comes out, the interest and qualified candidates will be invited to submit their applications online. It is advised that candidates willing and able to apply for the Nigerian Customs Service Recruitment should put their eyes to the ground so they would know when the registration commences.
